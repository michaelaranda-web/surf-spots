Surf Spots Around the World

As an exhilirating and accessible sport, it comes as no surprise that surfing has reached every corner of the world. Let's check out just a few of the beaches where guys and gals of all nationalities go to shred.

France

Located on the northwest coast of France, this picturesque surf location has a certain "je ne sais quoi" about it.

Taiwan

The warm waters of Taiwan help to give an equally warm welcome to new surfers. Wave at a local surfer catching a wave, and they may wave back.

South Africa

The right hand surf break at this famous beach attracts surfers from all over the world. And the view is alright, I suppose.

Australia

You won't find many other surf spots with a skyscraper horizon - one of the unique traits of Australia's Gold Coast.
